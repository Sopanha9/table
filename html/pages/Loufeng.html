Luo Feng was born in the city of "303" in 2038. He was not an ordinary person from birth: because of a genetic fracture in his genes, Luo Feng became stronger, nimble, hardy and fast. His physical and mental characteristics have increased a thousand fold. Now he could not only develop much faster than his peers, but Luo Feng also gained the ability to move objects in space.
Story Abilities Personality
From a poor background, he becomes a powerful cosmic warrior, exploring galaxies and fighting ancient beings. Spirit energy control, blade techniques, interstellar combat. Determined, ambitious, never gives up.

Nine Tribulations Tome (九劫秘典) 🌌 Description: A top-tier body cultivation technique that makes Luo Feng’s physical body extremely strong, allowing him to regenerate quickly and withstand powerful attacks. 🌌 Power: This technique allows him to reach immortal-level strength and become nearly indestructible.

Time-Space Manipulation (时间与空间操控) ⏳ Description: Luo Feng gains control over time and space, allowing him to slow down enemies, teleport, and even freeze time momentarily. ⏳ Power: As he masters this ability, he can control the flow of time in battles, giving him an almost unbeatable advantage.

Star Tower Control (星塔掌控) 🏛️ Description: A cosmic-level weapon, the Star Tower, is one of the most powerful artifacts in the universe. Luo Feng gains control over it, using it to trap, crush, and annihilate enemies. 🏛️ Power: The tower can distort space, making it nearly impossible for enemies to escape once inside. It’s one of the strongest defensive and offensive treasures in the universe.
