Xiao Yan is the main protagonist of Battle Through the Heavens, and reincarnated from Earth, but the author did not give information about his life on Earth. He was once hailed as a genius as a child but was called trash ever since Yao Lao resided in the ring his mother gave to him before she died. He then later sets out on a journey to become stronger. At first, his goal was just to be able to defeat Nalan Yanran, but his final goal has risen much higher as his enemies are at the top of the "food-chain" and he realizes he will have to surpass them. His aim is to reach Dou Di ( Battle God ), which is the highest rank.

Story Abilities Personality
A martial artist searching for an ancient scroll. Fire-based attacks, fast reflexes. Brave, determined, strong-willed.

His Powerfull Skills

Buddha’s Fury Lotus (佛怒火莲) 🔥 Description: A terrifying fire-based attack that creates a massive lotus of flames. When unleashed, it causes a devastating explosion that burns everything in its path. 🔥 Power: It combines different types of flames, making it one of the strongest destructive abilities in the series.

Flame Mantra (焚决) 🔥 Description: A unique cultivation technique that allows Xiao Yan to absorb and refine different heavenly flames, continuously making him stronger.. 🔥 Power: The more flames he absorbs, the stronger he becomes. This technique is the foundation of his power.

Three Thousand Lightning Movement (三千雷动) ⚡ Description: A high-speed movement technique that lets Xiao Yan move at lightning speed, making it hard for enemies to catch or hit him. ⚡ Power: Not only does it increase his speed, but it also allows him to use surprise attacks and dodge deadly blows.
